## 2. What data is collected, and why

### 2.1 Server and access logs

My web server records limited technical information needed to operate and secure the Site — the date and time of requests, 
the pages or files requested, and browser user-agent strings. These logs do not contain personal data: no IP addresses and 
nothing that identifies you individually. They are therefore outside the scope of UK GDPR, though I mention them here for transparency.

### 2.2 Content delivery and security network

The Site is served through Cloudflare, which acts as a processor on my behalf.
Cloudflare receives and processes visitor connection data (including IP address
and request details) at its global network in order to route traffic, cache
content, and protect the Site against malicious activity. Cloudflare processes
this data under its Data Processing Addendum.

- **Purpose:** secure and reliable delivery of the Site.
- **Lawful basis:** legitimate interests (Article 6(1)(f) UK GDPR).
- **More information:** Cloudflare's privacy practices are described at
  [https://www.cloudflare.com/privacypolicy/](https://www.cloudflare.com/privacypolicy/) and its sub-processors are listed
  at [https://www.cloudflare.com/gdpr/subprocessors/](https://www.cloudflare.com/gdpr/subprocessors/).


### 2.3 Analytics

The Site uses home rolled analytics tool to understand how visitors use it. [This is the data
